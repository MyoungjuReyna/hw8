Q. Without using relative or absolute positioning,

what is another way to organize elements on a page?

- Answer : Float

The float element is very useful to incorporate an image with a block of text. As the web designer you can decide to float the image to the left or to the right.

Q. Why I chose the colors?

I found these color combination from Color Hunt. I wanted to make green as a background. It's because Green was the predominant color in the forest, so the color green has a restful effect. In addition, green makes people feel comfortable and relieves their stress. Yellow and green are complementary colours, so the effect is striking. And the pink is also one of comeplementary color. In a short, the reason that I chose these color combination is that I wanted to emphasize the calm and necessary parts of the site.

The Aside Section

The aside can represent secondary content outside of an article, that is considered secondary to the main content in the article. The aside can be cool typography designs, quotes, advertising, etc... The aside can also be utlized within an article, although in this instance there should be some parallel to the content of the article.
